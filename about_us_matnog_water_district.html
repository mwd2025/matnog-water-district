About Matnog Water District

The Matnog Water District (MWD), Matnog, Sorsogon came into existence on November 8, 1982 through Matnog Municipal Council Resolution No. 48, transferring full power, authority and full responsibility in managing the water system from the Municipality of Matnog. Having passed all requirements, and in consonance with Presedential Decree (PD) No. 198, the local Water District Administration (LWUA) issued Conditonal Certificate of Conformance (CCC) No. 263 dated November 7, 1986, thus, making MWD qualified for financial and technical assistance from LWUA, through loans that are essential for its development and expansion of water facilities. It is categorize under "Category D" water district (WD).

Vision

We envision MATNOG WATER DISTRICT as a big, dynamic, credible and financially sustainable water service provider that can maintain the quality and sufficiency of its water with personnel who are highly-trained, courteous, dependable and reliable united by a just, responsive, efficient and effective service.

Mission

A Water District delivering adequate, potable water supply to the people of Matnog managed by highly competent, dynamic human resources always committed for excellence in service to every household, Government institution as well as Commercial establishments and other entreprenuers contributing to an economically progressive Municipality of MATNOG.
